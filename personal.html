About Me

I enjoy table tennis (a big fan of Sun Yingsha and Wang Chuqin), tennis, and music. My playlist often rotates between Debussy and Chopin on one side, and Faye Wong, Dou Wei, Cui Jian, Lo Ta-yu and Lisa Ono on the other. I’m also drawn to painting, visual arts, and anything that sparks creativity— including discovering new places through travel. I’m from Panzhihua, Sichuan, China, home to the Ertan Hydropower Station— one of China’s major hydroelectric dams built on the Yalong River.

Macro-Financial Review

[View Cover]
As a core member of the Macro-Financial Review team under the leadership of Norbert Fiess and Erik Feyen, co-authored sections of the Macro-Financial Review, a semi-annual internal publication of the World Bank Group (WBG), covering macro-financial risks across multiple countries.

  • Macro-Financial Review, Third Issue (April 2018): Co-authored sections analyzing macro-financial risks in 44 countries (pp. 11-48).
  • Macro-Financial Review, Fourth Issue (October 2018): Co-authored sections on macro-financial risks (pp. 17-44), including a special topic on blockchain applications and use cases (pp. 80-89).
